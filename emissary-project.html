Emissary Project

SlingShot's mission is to help more people travel further. We believe the world can be a more inclusive, peaceful place - and the fastest way to get there is by building bridges to connect cultures.

 

Interested in telling stories to promote this vision?

  • Writing about land mines in Cambodia?
  • Building schools in Afghanistan?
  • Taking photos of entrepreneurs in Cuba?
  • Exploring the backcountry of the Canadian Rockies?

We're open to your ideas, from simple to complex, small to large. We specialize in moonshots.

 

Drop us a line and we'll work some magic behind the scenes to give you the resources to make it happen.

You choose the adventure, we help make it happen!
